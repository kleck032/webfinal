Why Goldendoodles Are The Best!

Why Goldendoodles?

Goldendoodles are known for being friendly and great for families.


Goldendoodles have been successful guide dogs, therapy dogs, and sniffer dogs (such as sniffing out for a peanut allergy). They do not, however, make great watch dogs.

Thinking About Adopting?

For first time owners, adopting a Goldendoodle is a great option. They are both easy to train and do not have aggressive traits. A few things to take note of before considering adoption are both your current living situation and how much time you are home.


Goldendoodles need to be properly socialized in order to be comfortable around other dogs. In addition, if left alone for too long, Goldendoodles can suffer from separation anxiety.
